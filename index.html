About Us

Kimberly Dunham is the CEO of Business Navigation Solutions. After developing a passion for Human Resources and Leadership in her career she transitioned from corporate executive work to human resources. One of the key take-aways she has learned from working within the HR space is that you see it all. The good, the bad and the ugly in managers and employees alike. Overcoming the bad and ugly is a challenge she loves to tackle and learnings from the good and great is what she brings to her clients each day.

​

Kimberly has a bachelor’s degree in business administration and a master’s degree in human resource management.

Our Vision

The vision for Business Navigation Solutions is based on a passion and belief that all businesses need access to professional Human Resource knowledge, skills and tools that are typically afforded to large businesses. Most small businesses and startup companies characteristically don’t have the resources to have full time HR professionals on staff. We have developed the right processes, procedures and expertise to provide human resource services focused on small businesses and startup companies.

Kimberly Dunham - Founder
