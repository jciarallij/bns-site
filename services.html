Services

Your human resources are the key ingredient to the success and growth of any organization. What are your key human resources, talent acquisition or management concerns? How are you ensuring that you are getting the right people for your organization? How are you developing them to bring out their greatest potential and plans for the future? Are you looking to improve your hiring and employee development practices? Develop a succession plan? Develop your employees for the continued growth of your company? Let our services assist you in your endeavors.

Recruiting

We can post your positions, perform the initial screening of the applicants and present you the candidates that meet your criteria allowing you to focus on the business.

Training and Development

Employees with access to training and development programs have the advantage over employees in companies without. The investment in training that a company makes shows the employees they are valued and creates a supportive workplace.

New Hire Orientation/Onboarding

Employee onboarding isn’t just crucial for your company, it’s critical in the development of your staff. As your new hires become familiar with your company culture, resources, and their job roles, they’ll build relationships, grow their networks and become high-productivity employees. Let us create an onboarding experience that engages the employee Day 1.

Employee and Policy & Procedure Handbooks

We customize employee handbooks, welcome guides, policy & procedure manuals covering federal and state legal compliance. We also do corporate code of conduct handbooks to ensure your employees are well informed of your standards and guidelines.

Performance Management

Performance Management goes well beyond the appraisal. We believe the appraisal is essential for staff growth but should also be integrated with roles and responsibilities, goals, career pathing and development planning which is in alignment with company objectives. We can help you to design and implement the performance appraisal and management program for your company.

Organizational Design and Development (OD)

OD improves the performance of organizations and its people by addressing root-cause obstacles to achieving high performance status. OD initiatives can be the lever that turns business aspirations into reality. This can be done by aligning company goals and objectives, organizational structure, succession planning, etc.
